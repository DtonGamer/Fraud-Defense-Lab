Case Study: The Campaign Fraud Detection

During an active influencer campaign, our monitoring detected that engagement spiked to 5,000 likes in the first hour, then dropped to nearly zero. Further analysis revealed the influencer had purchased bot engagement to inflate apparent campaign success. Link tracking showed 8,000 clicks but zero conversions despite a compelling offer.

Result: Client stopped payment on remaining installments, saving $45,000. Influencer was removed from approved partner list, preventing future fraud.
